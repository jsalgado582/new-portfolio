Who I Am

I am a self-taught Web designer and SEO with real world experience as a freelancer and corporate employee. I am passionate about web design because as a task oriented person, I prefer to express my creativity through my work rather my words. Web Design allows me to connect with clients on a personal level and dig through what they think they want to create what they need.

I am a former Army Combay Medic (68W) with a STEM Bachelor's Degree in Environmental Biology from Georgia Gwinnet College. I practice Brazilian Jiujitsu, I'm an avid traveler, amateur environmentalist, Real Estate investor, and Audible enthusiast.

Fun Fact: I am have done a 3 month roadtrip to almost every National Park in the USA in my Jeep Wrangler with my beagle.
